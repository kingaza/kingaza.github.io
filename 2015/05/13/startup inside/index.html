内部创业

  • 创业
  • 项目管理

2014年夏天的达沃斯论坛。李克强总理在开幕全会上致辞表示，要破除一切束缚发展的体制机制障碍，让每个有创业意愿的人都有自主创业空间，让创新创造的血液在全社会自由流动。借改革创新的东风，在960万平方公里大地上掀起大众创业、草根创业新浪潮。
有国家政策法规的支持，全国上下到处都能看到创业的报道，而那些一夜暴富的故事更是激励了许许多多的年轻人。但对很多人来说，即使有创业的冲动，但因为各种主观客观的原因，始终走不出那一步。有时候想，如果公司内部允许创业是否是一个折中的方法呢？不过，估计大部分的公司都未必有这样子的胸襟。

之前西门子搞了个金点子创意大赛，假设有100万人民币的资金，如何把它用到所在部门的业务创新中。然后我就写了一篇东西，不过也没有然后了。全文抄录如下。

The only product of my company is MR Scanners; therefore, our daily work focuses on writing and maintaining software components for MR system in the common platform of Syngo. As a consequence of such a situation, our R&D activities might be limited in a narrow range.

Of course, innovation is always encouraged. When good new ideas come out, we propose a plan with work effort and time line, and the process status will be updated periodically. However, the real situation is that, the work efforts of colleagues are always occupied by other official projects, so that the innovation process is not guarantee.

To solve this problem, I propose a plan of setting up organization to improve innovation activities. If 10,000,000 RMB is available, many minor projects can be supported very well. The funding will be injected into a capital pool, which is controlled by a committee. In this committee, the members, including department manager, VP for PLM, technology experts, and advisers from other departments, have voting right, to decide if (1) approving projects and (2) providing funding. The funding will be used to pay for (1) material cost (2) the working time of the employee, (3) salary of intern if there is

In this way, an employee submits an application with his idea to the committee. Then the committee launch a meeting to discuss and decide if to approve this application, and how much funding.
So, the application is a very important stage, and must be prepared seriously. For each project application, the applicant must provide these information in details:

  • Technology feasibility
  • Reasonable budget plan
  • Risk management
    Once this application is approved, the applicant will play as a project manager to push his idea into reality. When the project is finished, the committee will evaluate the value of achievement from this project. In this value evaluation, the committee shall take in to account the factors of output quality, work effort and difficulty, influence, and even the team organization.
    Then, if software department or even other departments, who think that these achievements from the innovation projects are valuable and also wants to make use of these achievements , must pay according to the evaluated cost. Obviously, these funding will be injected into the capital pool back.

If this plan goes on well, our company can get more valuable products, and the employees not only improve their technology skills, but also build up their experience of project management. This means more chances in their career lives. And furthermore, they do their own things, which could be very interesting and exciting.
At the end, these facts can judge if this plan is successful after running for a duration:

  1. Finished projects with valuable output
  2. Benefits to products
  3. Better if money increases
